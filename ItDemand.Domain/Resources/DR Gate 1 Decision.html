All Demand that requirees additional scrutiny will go through the Demand Committee. The demand Committee will either approve the Demand to Proceed Locally, Proceed as a Fast Track project or to Proceed as a Large Project. Alternativly the demand Committee may request that the Demand return for a second review after additional information or decisions are available.

Demand Committee - Core Members:

  • PMO (Chair)
  • Security
  • Architecture & Applications
  • Infrastructure
  • Subject Matter Experts (as required)
  • Leadership Team Members (including Regional Buisness Unit IT Directors)
